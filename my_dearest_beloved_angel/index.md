# 00 - Prologue

## *What is love?*
A rhetorical question pondered on a child's mind. Love can't be put by words that can be understood by an 8 year old who's watching the world around him. There's many forms of love; A love for family, a love for friendship, many more. But there's a form of love that everybody seems to chase into. A *Romantic Love*. A kind of love where two people have deep interest and care for each other. This kid have an idea what romantic love is like thanks to media, but never have an idea what it feels like, as he hasn't experienced it yet. In this story, he will, eventually. But he's understanding of love is so fucked up.



Everyone wants to be loved romantically, but no one ever talks what makes love actually *romantic.* You might say "Oh its about giving gifts and taking care of each other yeah". Sure it may be that way, but people never talks about the negative sides, the awkward moments, the painful misunderstandings between relationships. The negativity is what makes the love grow. But in this world we live in. Everybody is obsessed with the idea of being *perfect.*

Perfect date, perfect faces, perfect everything. That's where everyone is focused at. While it isn't wrong to wanting to be perfect, wanting to be perfect at all things is wrong. We give all of our efforts to all thing that doesn't even benefit us. Focus is a limited resource we have. And we spend it on wrong things. Just like we spend money on useless stuff that eventually we run out of money and regret it.

The meaning of love in modern world is fucked up. Really fucked up. And this kid will experience it in wrong way. He will be obsessed. He will be desperate. And the person who he so called "Angel" will die in his own hands. Staining not just only his favorite cloth, but also his reality, his views, his everything.

He will suffer.
